Lorina Marshall-Blake

Lorina Marshall Blake is another client I had the pleasure of creating a logo for during my internship for The Wilson Concept. Lorina describes herself as an out-of-the-box person and wanted her brand to make a statement. She was very fond of golds, teals, and rose colors. She wanted her brand to make a bold yet feminine appearance. At first, I wanted to create a seamless ribbon-like logo for Lorina. It was bold and had the colors she liked, but it was a bit too intricate as a logo. Instead, I decided to incorporate a rose into her logo design and I was very pleased with how it turned out. I also created some business card mock-ups to go along with the new logo and following the outline given to me from a project brief which included the tagline.
